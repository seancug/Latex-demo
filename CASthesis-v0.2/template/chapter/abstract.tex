\begin{abstract}
复杂介质波动方程数值模拟越来越越受到地球物理学界的关注，各向异性介质和双相各向异性介质已然成为当今研究的热点和前言课题。选此课题，一来是为了解关于复杂介质波动方程正演的前沿发展，二来为掌握地球物理研究所必须的研究方法，为以后的发展做铺垫。\par本文首先给出了交错网格差分定义，并且推导得到交错网格空间和时间偶阶差分计算公式。结合各向异性介质的本构方程、运动微分方程、几何方程推导得出了应力-速度弹性波方程，并得出应力-速度弹性波的交错网格差分格式。对各向异性交错网格差分格式的稳定性进行讨论。\par对各向异性介质进行正演计算，具体讨论了正演计算时的震源函数及其加载方式。对边界条件应用效果及优缺点进行讨论。对交错网格有限差分格式计算时的频散进行讨论，并且给出相应矫正的措施。对各向同性均匀介质和各向异性均匀介质，进行正演数值计算，并且对比研究，得出qP波和qS波弹性波的特征。对各向异性介质层状介质进行正演计算，观察和研究qP和qS反射波及转换波的传播特征。\par推导得到双相各向异性介质的速度-应力弹性波方程。对双相各向异性介质均匀介质和双相各向异性层状介质进行正演计算，观察和研究双相各向异性均匀介质弹性波的特征。及双相各向异性层状介质反射波和转换波的传播特征。

\keywords{交错网格,各向异性,双相,正演}
\end{abstract}



\begin{englishabstract}
In recent years, the wave-field simulated of complex media become more and more popular in seismology. I choose this as graduation thesis in order to become aware of the development in the wave-field simulated of complex media. Moreover, I can acquire the basic skills of doing the research after this thesis.\par
In this paper, we first present the definition of the staggered-grid. We also derive the even order differential formula of staggered-grid in space and time. We derive the velocity-stress equations based on constitutive equations, differential equations of motion, geometric equation. What’s more, we present the formula of velocity-stress staggered-grid finite-difference by applied the even order differential formula of staggered-grid and discuss the stability of velocity-stress staggered-grid finite-difference.\par
We discuss the source function and how to add the source when we are simulating the wave propagation in the anisotropic media. We study the strength and weakness of attenuation boundary condition. In this paper, we also analyze the numerical dispersion in wave-field simulation and percent some methods to correct it. In order to distinguish difference between quasi-P/quasi-S wave and P/S wave, we simulation the wave-fields in homogeneous anisotropic media and homogeneous isotropic media Moreover, we simulate the wave-field in layered anisotropic media to observe the characteristic of reflection of quasi-P/quasi-S wave.\par
For two-phase anisotropic media (poroelastic anisotropic media), we give the velocity-stress equations and simulate the wave-field in homogeneous two-phase anisotropic media and layered two-phase anisotropic media, in order to observe the characteristic of wave in them.

\englishkeywords{staggered-grid   anisotropic   two-phase (poroelastic)   simulate}
\end{englishabstract}
